# Recipe & Meal Planner App

A comprehensive web application for managing recipes and planning meals, built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Open the app**: Navigate to `recipe-planner.html` in your browser
2. **Or view online**: Upload to your GitHub pages and access via your repository URL

## ✨ Features

### 📚 Recipe Management
- **Add New Recipes**: Click "Add Recipe" to create recipes with ingredients, instructions, prep/cook times
- **Edit Recipes**: Click the "Edit" button on any recipe card
- **Delete Recipes**: Remove recipes you no longer need
- **Categorize**: Organize by breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert, snack, or appetizer
- **Dietary Tags**: Mark recipes as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto, etc.

### 📅 Meal Planner
- **Weekly View**: Navigate through weeks with arrow buttons
- **Drag & Drop**: Click on meal slots to assign recipes to specific days and meal types
- **Smart Suggestions**: The app suggests appropriate recipes based on meal type (breakfast recipes for breakfast slots, etc.)

### 🛒 Shopping List Generator
- **Auto-Generate**: Click "Generate from Meal Plan" to create shopping lists from your weekly meals
- **Organized Categories**: Items are automatically sorted into Produce, Meat & Seafood, Dairy, Pantry, etc.
- **Quantity Tracking**: Shows how many times each ingredient appears across recipes
- **Check Off Items**: Mark items as completed while shopping

### 🔍 Advanced Search & Filtering
- **Quick Search**: Search recipes by name or ingredient from the main recipes page
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter by dietary restrictions, maximum prep time, and more
- **Category Filtering**: View only recipes from specific categories

## 💾 Data Persistence

All your data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage:
- Recipes are preserved between sessions
- Meal plans are saved and restored
- Shopping lists persist until you generate new ones

## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Meal Planning**: Start by adding several recipes, then use the meal planner to assign them to specific days
2. **Shopping Lists**: Plan a full week of meals before generating your shopping list for maximum efficiency
3. **Recipe Organization**: Use categories and dietary tags to make finding recipes easier
4. **Batch Cooking**: Use the notes field to add tips about batch cooking or meal prep

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **No Backend Required**: Runs entirely in the browser
- **Local Storage**: All data saved locally, no external servers needed
- **Modern CSS**: Uses CSS Grid, Flexbox, and custom properties
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: No frameworks required, fast and lightweight
- **Font Awesome Icons**: Beautiful icons throughout the interface
